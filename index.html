GivemeO App User Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from the GivemeO app, please follow the steps below:

In-App Deletion

Open the GivemeO app, go to the settings page, and select the "Delete Account" option.

Email Request

If you prefer, you can also request data deletion by sending an email to ashish.paikrao@dynamisch.co with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include your account details in the email.

We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact our support team at the above email address.
